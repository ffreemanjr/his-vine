We Are Happy You Are here

We believe that the Everlasting Gospel should be preached to all people, nom matter who you are, what your background is, what your lifestyle is, or what you may or may not think about yourself. We believe that Jesus came and died for everyone, including you, and that is all that matters when it comes to fellowship.
