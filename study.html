Welcome to Bible Study

Bible Study Archive

Here at HIS Vine, our mission is to equip evryone desiring to learn God's Sovereign will for their lives, with practical solutions for application of health and spiritual principals.

For our Heavenly Father has commissioned us with a sacred duty: to submit to His Sovereign will, and reflect HIS character in our daily lives.

To begin your study, please click on the study title to open. Once opened, select the topic to study. It is advisable to begin from the first study and work through the others to get a good understanding of the lessons.

Some of the lessons require Adobe Reader. If you do not have it installed, please click here to download it now. Once in the lesson, you will be able to save or print your study. Please use the "Back" button in your browser to return to the study lesson.

Romans, Galatians, Daniel, and Revelation are all video studies. Please make sure you have your Bible with you along with a notebook to take notes. During any of the study, if you have any questions, please feel free to CONTACT US!

Study on Ephesians

Ephesus was a major city in Asia Minor with over a quarter of a million people living there at one time. This study focuses on Apostle Paul's letter to the Ephesians, whom he spent time ministering to.

Begin Study

Study on Romans

Paul's letter to the Romans is believed to be the longest of the epistles he wrote. It establishes the majority of church doctrine regarding sin, salvation, sanctification, and glorification. This is an important study for all Christians.

Begin Study

Study on Daniel

Prophecy is one way that God has used to inform HIS people of coming events. The book of Daniel, as written by Daniel the Prophet, is the first of two prophetic books of the Bible that depict end times events. This is an important study for all Christians today.

Begin Study

Study on Revelation

Revelation, the second of the two end time prophetic books of the Bible, was written by the Apostle John while exiled on the island of Patmos. It is the inspired revelation of Jesus Christ, and provides waymarks for Christians and focus on the Three Angels' messsages.

Begin Study

Life of Christ

This lesson introduces all Bible students to Jesus and the life He lived while on earth. It is an important study in that it shows that Jesus came to show us the Father and how to live a victorious life through His faith. His life and ministry are lessons for all Christians.
