Articles of The Constitution

Thank you for taking the time to read our Constitution and By-Laws. We serve a God who is a God of order; and as such, we hope that through this document, we would set the direction of order as God expects from His people. As you read each article, please locate the button in each one to open a PDF copy of the "Article" on your screen. Once opened, you can download or print each document.

As always please feel free to contact us if you have any questions about any parts of the document you read. Click here to get in touch with us.

May God continue to guide and guard you as you endeavor to live for Him each day!

The Constitution

Article 1: Name

Section 1. This spiritual body of Christ shall be known as HIS VINE FREE SEVENTH-DAY ADVENTIST CHURCH.

Section 2.The use of the name “Free Seventh-day Adventist,” as the privilege and prerogative of this spiritual body, was and is fully recognized by court order dated July 6, 1923 by the Chatham Superior Court in and for Chatham County, State of Georgia.
