Standing on the Foundation

Thank you for taking the time to read our Constitution and By-Laws. We serve a God who is a God of order; and as such, we hope that through this document, we would set the direction of order as God expects from His people. As you read each article, please locate the button in each one to open a PDF copy of the "Article" on your screen. Once opened, you can download or print each document.

As always please feel free to contact us if you have any questions about any parts of the document you read. Click here to get in touch with us.

May God continue to guide and guard you as you endeavor to live for Him each day!

ByLaws of the Constitution

Tithes and Offerings

SECTION 1. The Church is to receive tithes as a self-supporting ministry to support the work of the pastor/elder, and the work of evangelism world-wide. It is also to receive offerings to support the day-to-day expenses of the church. The Church is God’s Storehouse and giving above and beyond the requirements of God as set forth in Article 13 of the Statement of Beliefs is to be done according to the unction of the Holy Spirit given to the individual giver. 2Corinthians 9:6, 7.

Read More

Books and Records

SECTION 1. The Church will keep and maintain complete books and records of accounting. The Church will comply with all State and Federal regulations as it is required to do so in this regard that men may see our good works and glorify our Father which is in Heaven. Romans 13:2-6; 1Peter 2:13-16.

Read More

Officers and Their Duties

SECTION 1. The Officers of the Church will be the Chairman—Pastor, Vice Chairman—Head Elder, a Secretary, and a Treasurer.

SECTION 1. The Chairman will work closely with the other members of the Executive Board to protect, promote, and provide for the interests of the Church as led by the Holy Spirit. He will preside at all Board Meetings and sessions. The vice-Chairman will preside at meetings held when the Chairman—Pastor is absent.
