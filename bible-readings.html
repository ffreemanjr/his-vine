Bible Reading for the Home Circle —1914 Edition

HE MESSAGES that we have received from heaven are true and faithful. When one man strives to bring in new theories, which are not the truth, the ministers of God should bear clear warning against these theories, pointing out where, if received, they would lead the people of God. Those who have received the light of present truth should not be easily deceived, and readily led from the true path into strange paths. The watchmen are to be wide-awake to discern the outcome of all specious reasoning; for serious errors will be brought in to lead the people of God astray.

When men come in who would move one pin or pillar from the foundation which God has established by His Holy Spirit, let the aged men who were pioneers in our work speak plainly, and let those who are dead speak also, by the reprinting of their articles in our periodicals. Gather up the rays of divine light that God has given as He has led His people on step by step in the way of truth. This truth will stand the test of time and trial.

Part I: The Bible

How to study and understand the bible.

Study on Ephesians

Ephesus was a major city in Asia Minor with over a quarter of a million people living there at one time. This study focuses on Apostle Paul's letter to the Ephesians, whom he spent time ministering to.

Study on Romans

Paul's letter to the Romans is believed to be the longest of the epistles he wrote. It establishes the majority of church doctrine regarding sin, salvation, sanctification, and glorification. Because of this, it appears first among all his writings because of this.

Study On Daniel

Prophecy is one way that God has used to inform HIS people of coming events. The book of Daniel, as written by Daniel the Prophet is one of two prophetic books of the Bible that depict end times events.

Study on Revelation

Revelation, the second of the two end time prophetic books of the Bible, was written by the Apostle John while exiled on the island of Patmos. It is the inspired revelation of Jesus Christ, and provides waymarks for Christians and focus on the Three Angels' messsages.

The Life of Christ

This lesson introduces all Bible readers and students to Jesus and the life He lived while on earth. It is an important study in that it shows that Jesus came to show us the Father and how to live a victorious life through His faith.
