Biblical Footprints

In addition to the fundamental truth in our faith, the great doctrines of the spiritual church, there are additional footprints we, as believers, need to have a complete understanding of in order for the life of Christ to be reflected in our daily lives. These are underlying principles that define our faith as Free Seventh-day Adventists that are essential for proper reading and comprehension of the Bible and the writings of Ellen G. White. One of the earmarks of being a Free Seventh-day Adventist is our adherence to the historical principles of our faith as established by the pioneers. Below are the additional principles of our faith.
