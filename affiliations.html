Who Are We Associated With?

Certificate of Membership

His Vine Free Seventh-Day Adventist Church is a member of The Free Seventh-Day Adventists World Assembly. Below is our membership Certificate. Please contact us for any questions you may have about this document.

The organization was founded in 1912 by J. W. Manns. He bought property from Cora S. Cassels on November 29th 1912. Next, he made arrangements for the church to purchase the property from him on October 15th 1915. Then, the church borrows money from the bank Dec 03, 1915.

Free Seventh-day Adventists are a union of like-minded churches and ministries upholding the old landmarks of biblical Adventism where the local congregation is its own sovereign entity under God. We trace our foundational roots back to the early twentieth century southern revival movement of Elder J. W. Manns and Lewis C. Sheafe within the African-American community; they courageously introduced this connection of believers to the world in 1916. We are dedicated to the continuation of the revivals which were initiated by our forefathers and the ecclesiastical heritage which they handed down. We are a compassionate, caring, and prayerful community of believers committed to relieving the sufferings of humanity and preparing a people for eternity. We seek to aid mankind in the areas of health, true education, and eternal life in Christ Jesus.
